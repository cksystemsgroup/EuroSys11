Conference Venue

Eurosys 2011 will take place at:
Universitätsaula Salzburg
Max-Reinhardt-Platz
5020 Salzburg
Austria
The main conference venue is the Aula of the University of Salzburg. The Aula is located in the pedestrians-only historic city center of Salzburg across the street of the world-famous Festspielhaus. All major attractions of the old town as well as many restaurants and cafes are within walking distance.

Getting to the conference site


View Eurosys 2011 in a larger map

  • From Salzburg Airport
    Take the bus 8 to stop "Herbert von Karajan Platz". The trip takes approximately 15 minutes.

Workshop Site

The workshop site is the Faculty of Law of the University of Salzburg. The Faculty of Law is also located in the pedestrians-only historic city center of Salzburg just around the corner of the main conference site.

Address: Faculty of Law
Churfürststraße 1
5020 Salzburg


Reception Site

The reception will be held at the “Neue Residenz”, which is a recently renovated 400-year old building within walking distance of the workshop site.

Address: Neue Residenz
Residenzplatz 9
5020 Salzburg

VMware Banquet

The banquet dinner will be held in the evening of the second conference day at the Stieglkeller restaurant within walking distance of the main conference site.

Address: Stiegelkeller
Festungsgasse 10
5020 Salzburg

Google Guided Tour

The 1000-year-old “Hohen Salzburg” fortress is one of the largest existing 11th century fortresses in Europe. The fortress is within walking distance of the main conference site.

Address: Fortress Hohensalzburg
Mönchsberg 34
5020 Salzburg
