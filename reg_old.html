Registration Information

Below are the registration fees for EuroSys 2011. You are eligible for a reduced registration fee if:

  • you are a member of either ACM or EuroSys (*),
  • you are a student (**) enrolled at a university or college,
  • you pay the Eurosys member fee together with the registration fee.

(*) Please note that you will be asked for your ACM/EuroSys member ID if you select a "Member" ticket.
(**) Student registration requires a written letter on university letter head, with the statement that the registered person is a full-time PhD student at the time of the conference, to be signed by the university professor who is the student's advisor. This letter should be sent to the following email address: eurosys2011@gmail.com.

Important: Early registration with reduced fees is possible until March 11, 2011. After that, fees for late registration apply.

If you have questions or problems with the registration process, please send email to: eurosys2011@gmail.com.
